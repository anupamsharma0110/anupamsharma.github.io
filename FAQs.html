DOES THE UTILITY PROVIDE CREDIT FOR THE PV POWER GOING TO THEIR GRID?


Yes. Utility will provide you the credit for the excess generation of power. The credited units can be used over throughout a year cycle.


WHAT DOES THE UTILITY PAY FOR THE PV KWH?


Every region has different rates for the PV KWh. In the state of Gujarat, the utility pays you Rs 6 per unit of energy.


IS THERE A LIMIT ON THE AMOUNT THEY WILL ACCEPT?


No there is no limit on the amount they will accept. On the contrary, they have defined a limit on the production. A solar system owner cannot produce more than 80% of the connected load in his home.


CAN YOU CARRY CREDIT OVER FROM ONE BILLING CYCLE TO THE NEXT?


Yes! You can carry credit over one billing cycle to next.
